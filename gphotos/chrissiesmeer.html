Birds of Chrissiesmeer

Chrissiesmeer is a wetland area of Mpumalanga province in South Africa. Chrissiesmeer is best known for its incredible birding. The Chrissie Pans supports 287 species of birds, with a particularly wide variety of waterfowl lured by the numerous lakes and pans.
