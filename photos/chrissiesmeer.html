Birds of Chrissiesmeer

Chrissiesmeer is a small town situated in Msukaligwa Local Municipality, in a wetland area of Mpumalanga province in South Africa, on the northern banks of the eponymous Lake Chrissie. Chrissiesmeer is best known for its incredible birding. The Chrissie Pans supports 287 species of birds, with a particularly wide variety of waterfowl lured by the numerous lakes and pans.
